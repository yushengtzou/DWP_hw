Academic Seminar: Dr. Yu-Ting Tseng (Alumnus of Class of '97) at the Department of Life Sciences, National Cheng Kung University on January 12th, 2023.

2022-12-28

        The Zhang Songbin Children's Education Fund Open LetterZhang Songbin, a professor in our Department of Life Sciences, has been serving our school since 2010. In terms of teaching, he is highly creative and profound, and also dedicated to guiding the growth and development of his students, earning their admiration and love. In terms of research, Professor Zhang has participated in research that has been published twice in the top journal NATURE, which is a remarkable achievement. In addition, since August 2020, Professor Zhang has served as the Vice Dean of Student Affairs of our school, assisting in the organization of large-scale events such as the National Intercollegiate Athletic Games. His hands-on attitude is truly admirable and impressive. Professor Zhang has also served as the Vice President of the Department of Life Sciences Alumni Association for four years, contributing greatly to the graduates and students of the Department of Life Sciences. However, he left us at the end of March this year, and I believe that those who know him were shocked and regretful. In appreciation of Professor Zhang's contributions to our school, our department intends to turn your gratitude and thanks into practical action, conveying warmth and care to his children and assisting them with the educational expenses they need to grow.
