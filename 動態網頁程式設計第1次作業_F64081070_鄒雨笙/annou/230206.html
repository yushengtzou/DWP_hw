Add/Drop Procedures for the Second Semester of Academic Year 111-2 (2/13-17; 3/1-6)

2022-10-05

        Add/Drop period (fixed every semester) at the department office: Week 1 (Department office course selection): Acceptance of transfer students, students changing departments, students returning from leave, and students extending graduation date. Week 3 (Special circumstances course selection, also the last phase of department office course selection): Acceptance of closed courses, teacher changes, and class schedule changes. (Week 2 is the student's course selection period, and you can select courses on the course selection system yourself!) *Add/Drop period will continue until the classroom capacity is full, but it is not first come, first served. Add/Drop form: Department of Life Sciences website>Undergraduate>Undergraduate related forms (all forms required for freshmen to seniors are available here). Add/Drop procedure (Paper-based add/drop requests are only accepted for physical classes; paper-based and online add/drop requests are accepted for online classes): Paper-based: Department office provides paper form or print from the above website ➔ Course instructor signature or stamp ➔ Submit the form to the department office. Online: There is no online course this semester. Only paper-based requests are accepted. *Before submitting the add/drop form to the department office, please check if the form is completed.
