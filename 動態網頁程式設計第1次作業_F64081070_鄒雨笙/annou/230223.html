Notification of Interview for the Master's Program in Life Science, Academic Year 2023-2024

2022-10-05

        Each interview will last between 10-20 minutes, and the order of the interviews will be determined by the examination admission ticket number. The interview will take place at the Life Science Building laboratory and conference room on the Li-Hsin campus of National Cheng Kung University. Please download the interview data sheet and candidate recommendation letter from the department's website, print and complete them in detail or type them out. Two recommendation letters are required, and candidates can make copies themselves. The recommendation letters can be sent directly by the recommenders or can be submitted together with the application materials. In addition, please include a future research plan, a copy of your university transcript, and any past research experience (if applicable) and mail the documents by March 6th (Monday) (as evidenced by the postmark) to Dr. Huang Hao-ren, the director of the Department of Life Sciences. Please indicate the examination group and admission ticket number on the envelope. Please note that the written application materials will not be scored and are only for the interview committee to understand the candidate's learning situation and background information, so please provide a brief summary. If you are unable to attend the interview or wish to give up the interview, please notify us by letter or phone before March 7th (Tuesday). If you have any questions, please feel free to contact Ms. Chen at 06-2757575 ext. 58102.
